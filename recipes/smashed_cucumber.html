Smashed Cucumber

A delicious cucumber salad inspired by a Chinese recipe. A salad that is often found in Sichuanese restaurants. It is spicy, stuffed with garlic, fresh herbs, sesame oil. It's a delight even if the cucumber will suffer some damage.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 tablespoons of rice vinegar (you can choose Chinese black vinegar but you can find rice vinegar more easily)
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of sesame oil (grilled)
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of flaked chili pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 5 sprigs of coriander

Steps

  1. Finely chop the garlic.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the garlic and brown sugar.
  3. Add the vinegar to dissolve the sugar
  4. Then add the soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili pepper.(The marinade is done!)
  5. Tap the cucumber with a rolling pin to break up the fibers and obtain pieces. You can finish with a knife.
  6. In a salad bowl, mix the cucumber and marinade. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  7. Serve in a small container. finish the topping with sesame seeds and coriander leaves. Enjoy Note Spicy?! Adjust the chili to your liking!
